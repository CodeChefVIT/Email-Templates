Greetings from CodeChef-VIT!

It is a great pleasure to declare that, we at the DEVSOC’21 organising team were overwhelmed with the immense response received during the Idea submission phase of DEVSOC’21.

With over 2500 registrations and 250 idea submissions, it was a task of great deliberation and cautious evaluation to short list the top 69 teams. The shortlisted teams have been notified of their results.

If your team didn’t make it through, do not be disheartened yet, there still are many opportunities awaiting. You are eligible to take part in the AR Hunt, Social Media Challenges, Giveaways and the CS GO challenge. So participate and grab your chance at winning exciting prizes.

Stay tuned to our discord channel to be updated on the details of these events and win wonderful goodies.
